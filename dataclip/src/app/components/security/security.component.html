Security

We take security and privacy seriously.

  • Dataclip and its dependencies are scanned on a regular basis for security holes and known vulnerabilities in order to make your visit to our site as safe as possible.
  • All requests including submissions from your websites to Dataclip and API requests are encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.
  • All transactions are processed through a gateway provider. Credit card and transaction information are not stored or processed on our servers. 2checkout is the provider for purchases through the Dataclip website.
  • Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems.

Additionally we use providers with a focus on security including:

  • AWS
  • GitHub
  • 2checkout

See our privacy policy for more information. If you have questions about your data please reach out at info@dataclip.tech.
